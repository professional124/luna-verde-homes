Our Commitment to Sustainability

At Luna Verde Homes, we take pride in minimizing our ecological footprint while maximizing guest comfort. Here’s how we make a difference:

Eco-Friendly Construction

Our tiny homes are built using reclaimed wood, natural insulation, and non-toxic paints, ensuring minimal impact on the environment.

Renewable Energy

Solar panels and energy-efficient appliances power our homes, reducing reliance on fossil fuels.

Water Conservation

Rainwater harvesting systems and water-saving fixtures help us conserve precious water resources.

Waste Management

We promote recycling, composting, and responsible waste disposal throughout our retreat.
