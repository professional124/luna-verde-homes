Luxury under the waves

Our Story

Luna Verde means "Green Moon" in Spanish — a name that reflects our mission to blend elegance with eco-consciousness. Located off the coast of Brisbane, our off-grid underwater resort runs on renewable energy and offers comfort and adventure for families.
