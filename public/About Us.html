About Luna Verde Homes

At Luna Verde Homes, we are passionate about creating sustainable, beautiful tiny homes that sit right on the water. Our mission is to offer unforgettable experiences while promoting eco-friendly living and a deep connection to nature.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Luna Verde Homes grew from a love of tiny living and environmental stewardship. We combine modern design with responsible building practices to create homes that are both charming and sustainable.
